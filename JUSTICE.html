AssangeDAO's $JUSTICE Token

The Justice Governance Token ($JUSTICE) is an Ethereum-based ERC-20 token that governs the AssangeDAO. Its token address is 0x59d1e836F7b7210A978b25a855085cc46fd090B5. The total supply is 17,346,149,507 tokens, with none reserved for the DAO or paid to its founders or developers. $JUSTICE tokens were issued to contributors at a ratio of 1,000,000 per 1 ETH, and no further tokens can ever be minted.
